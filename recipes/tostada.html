Easy Chicken Tostadas

These easy chicken tostadas are just the thing for a busy weeknight. A fast spin on chicken tostadas, they can be done in about 20 minutes!

One of the best parts about this recipe is the simple ingredients that are easy to keep on hand.

You can use corn tortillas in the oven to make tostada shells, and cook your own chicken and then shred it, but I prefer to use store bought tostada shells and pre-cooked chicken to save time. Depending on the chicken you use, it still tastes great!

Ingredients

  • Tostada Shells
  • Canned/Frozen corn
  • Shredded Chicken
  • Salsa
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Black/Refried Beans
  • Shredded Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 450 degrees.
  2. In medium sauce pan, combine chicken, salsa, taco seasoning, corn, and black beans over medium heat.
  3. Cook until it is combined and hot, stirring occasionally. About 5 minutes.
  4. Add 1/2 cup of the mix to the top of each shell.
  5. Top with cheese on each, and place in oven for 5 minutes or until the cheese is completely melted.
  6. Top with cilantro and diced tomatoes.
