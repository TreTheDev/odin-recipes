Sesame Chicken

This Easy Sesame Chicken Recipe is a fast dinner to make and blows takeout out of the water!

Sesame Chicken is a crispy, sweet and tangy chicken recipe most often found in Chinese restaurants in the United States.

Although the chicken is often deep fried to make it crispy, this recipe skips the breading to make it faster, easier and a bit healthier.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds boneless, skinless, chicken thighs, cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 1 tbsp canola oil
  • 2 tsp Sesame seeds

    • For Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1tbsp grated ginger root
  • 4 garlic cloves minced
  • 1 tsp chili garlic sauce
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch

Steps

  1. Whisk together ingredients for sauce, set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large saute pan over medium high heat.
  3. When oil is hot, add the chicken. Sear until browned on one side, then flip and repeat on the second side, until chicken is cooked through, about 5 minutes per side.
  4. Add sauce, cook for 1-2 minutes, or until sauce has started to thicken and the chicken is coated in sauce.
  5. Garnish with sesame seeds and green onion, serve.
